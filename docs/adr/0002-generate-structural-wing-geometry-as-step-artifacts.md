# Generate Structural Wing Geometry as STEP Artifacts

The backend MVP generates three-dimensional wing structural geometry as downloadable STEP artifacts instead of returning a 1000-point JSON point cloud. The STEP artifact is a right-half-wing structural assembly with independent skin, front spar, rear spar, and rib solids, so users can open/export it in CAD and mesh-preprocessing tools; point clouds were rejected because they do not carry the topology needed for useful CAD exchange. STEP generation should use a CAD kernel such as CadQuery/OpenCascade rather than hand-written STEP text, with the skin outer surface following the CST section and internal members trimmed inside the skin; aerodynamic surface STEP artifacts remain a future separate output so structural geometry and clean external-flow geometry do not get mixed.
